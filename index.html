Clinical Neuropsychology in Southwest Ireland

Neuropsychology Health is a small clinical neuropsychology service based online and in southwest Ireland. Neuropsychology Health provides person-centred support for adults recovering from brain injuries and neurological conditions.

Services Include:

  • Neuropsychology Consultation
  • Neuropsychological Screening and Assessment
  • Memory and Dementia Assessments
  • Psychological Therapy and Neuropsychological Rehabilitation
  • Medico-Legal Services
  • Supervision, Teaching, and Consultancy
